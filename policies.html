Privacy Policy & Ad Policy – Blueverse Finance

At Blueverse Finance, your privacy is important. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in accordance with Google Ads and other advertising platforms.

1. Information We Collect

  • Non-personal information such as browser type, device, pages visited, and time spent on site.
  • Personal information only if voluntarily submitted (e.g., contact forms, newsletter signup).

2. How We Use Information

  • To improve website functionality and user experience.
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google Ads and other ad networks.
  • To respond to user inquiries and feedback.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies, Google Analytics, and other tracking technologies to analyze site traffic and improve our services. This helps in showing relevant ads and content to users.

4. Google Ads & Ad Platforms

Blueverse Finance may display ads from Google Ads, Adsterra, and other approved ad networks. We strictly follow their policies:

  • No encouraging clicks on ads.
  • No self-clicking or generating artificial impressions.
  • Ads are personalized based on interest and browsing behavior in compliance with Google policies.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Users are encouraged to read the privacy policy of each external site.

6. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect user data, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Consent

By using our website, you consent to the collection and use of information as outlined in this policy.

8. Changes to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us.
